Advancing Biofabrication

for a Healthier & Smarter Future

FABRICATE 2025 is an international conference dedicated to exploring the transformative potential of biofabrication and translational engineering, harnessing breakthroughs in bioprinting to drive the future of organ regeneration and personalized healthcare.Hosted at the prestigious IIT Madras Campus, this two-day event, co-organised by Next Big Innovation Labs® and IIT Madras, unites global experts to advance research, foster innovation, and address critical challenges in healthcare, biotechnology, and engineering.

In an era marked by rapid scientific advancements and complex healthcare needs, FABRICATE 2025 serves as a platform to drive meaningful conversations and collaborations.
